Avocoad Salad

Rate This Recipe:


Prep Time : 20min | Cooking Time : 1hr

6 Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Roasted beets and carrots
  • Watercress or arugula
  • Toasted pepitas
  • Tahini dressing
  • Salt and pepper

Tips for This Recipe!

  • Buy the avocados ahead of time.

    Say you’re craving guacamole. You head to the store to get what you need to make it – cilantro, red onion, tortilla chips, and a few perfectly ripe avocados. But when you get to the produce section, every last avocado feels like a rock. We’ve all been there: with avocados like that, making guac is completely out of the question. The same goes for this avocado salad! Make sure to buy your avocados a few days ahead of time so that they have a chance to ripen on your counter. When they’re soft, pop them in the fridge until you’re ready to make the recipe.

  • Roast the veggies the day before.

    When I make this salad, I always roast the beets and carrots a day or two in advance. That way, the salad is quick and easy to assemble. Plus, the veggies are cool. If they were hot from the oven, they’d wilt the tender greens.

  • Toast the pepitas.

    This step adds so much dimension to this avocado salad recipe, as it brings out the pepitas’ rich, nutty flavor. It’s super simple – just add the pepitas to a small skillet over low heat, and toast, stirring occasionally, until fragrant, about 1 minute. It’s so easy and so worth it!
